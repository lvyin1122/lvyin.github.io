Bits and Bytes

Bits

on/off

8 bits = 1 byte

Computer memory is just a large array of bytes

Byte-addressable

Fundamentally operate on bits

Base 10 & Base 2

Most significant bit (MSB) 最高有效位

Least significant bit (LSB) 最低有效位

Byte Values

8 bits: 0~255

Two’s Complement (二进制补码)

The negative number is the positive number inverted, plus one!

*Another trick: right-to-left original until reach a 1

Subtraction: convert to addition after complement

Multiplication: discard the overflow digits

Overflow

exceed max: back to smallest bit

exceed min: back to largest bit
